Counselling Service

Seek first to understand then to be understood. In my numerous roles I have become skilled at gauging how I might best help you. I have many options available and I will probably use a variety of these with you. These options will include my person entered grounding of non judgemental listening, empathy and understanding. With a more active element of CBT to help you retrain your thoughts. We might spend some time on compassion and mindfulness which I am a great advocate of. And possibly we will bring in elements of solution focused techniques, trauma work, or perhaps grief work- what ever it is I will bring my own style to it. I am based in Flackwell Heath between Beaconsfield and High Wycombe. Just off junction 3a of the M40 I work from my discreet garden office set beneath the trees in a non domestic location.
